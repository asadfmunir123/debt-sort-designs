Colour & Type Specification

The approved final direction: the Calm Vibrant colour palette, paired with the Warm Vibrant layout, copy and typography. This document is the single reference for engineering and design.

1 · Colour palette

Solid white page background throughout. Teal is the primary action colour; amber is the secondary / highlight colour. Light tints are for soft chip and badge backgrounds only - never large fields.
